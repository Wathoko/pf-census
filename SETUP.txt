# PF Census App — Setup Guide

## Overview

The app uses **Google Apps Script** as its backend — a free Google service that runs code inside your Google account. This means:

- No Google Cloud Console needed
- No API keys to manage
- Works from any phone browser
- Takes about 5 minutes to set up

---

## STEP 1 — Add the Script to Your Google Sheet

1. Open your Google Sheet:
   https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1-MpfWf3JqVAxdwo59eV6wd2Lv37YN-wa/edit

2. Click the menu: Extensions > Apps Script
   (A new browser tab will open — the Apps Script editor)

3. Select ALL the existing code (Ctrl+A) and DELETE it.

4. Open the file Code.gs from the C:\SHINSKI\PF-Census folder on your computer.
   Copy its entire contents and PASTE it into the Apps Script editor.

5. IMPORTANT: At the top of the pasted code, check the SHEET_NAME:
      const SHEET_NAME = 'Sheet1';
   Look at the tab name at the bottom of your Google Sheet.
   If it says something other than "Sheet1", update this value.

6. Click Save (Ctrl+S).

---

## STEP 2 — Deploy as a Web App

1. Click the Deploy button (top right) > New deployment

2. Click the gear icon next to "Type" > Select "Web app"

3. Set these options:
   - Description:  PF Census App
   - Execute as:   Me (your Google account)
   - Who has access:  Anyone

4. Click Deploy

5. Google will ask you to AUTHORIZE the app:
   - Click "Authorize access"
   - Choose your Google account
   - Click Advanced > Go to PF Census App (unsafe)
   - Click Allow

6. After authorization, copy the Web app URL shown:
   Example: https://script.google.com/macros/s/AKfycb.../exec

   SAVE THIS URL — you will need it in Step 4.

---

## STEP 3 — Open the App on Your Phone

OPTION A: Local Network (quick test)
   1. On your PC, open PowerShell and run:
         cd "C:\SHINSKI\PF-Census"
         python -m http.server 8080
   2. Find your PC's IP: run  ipconfig  > look for IPv4 Address (e.g. 192.168.1.5)
   3. On your phone, open Chrome and go to:  http://192.168.1.5:8080

OPTION B: GitHub Pages (free, works anywhere — recommended)
   1. Create a free account at https://github.com
   2. Create a new repository named  pf-census
   3. Upload all files from  C:\SHINSKI\PF-Census\
   4. Go to Settings > Pages > Source: main branch
   5. Your app URL will be:  https://yourusername.github.io/pf-census/
   6. Open this URL on your phone — done!

---

## STEP 4 — Connect the App to Your Sheet

1. Open the app on your phone/browser
2. You will see a setup screen asking for the Apps Script URL
3. Paste the URL you copied in Step 2
4. Tap "Connect to Google Sheet"
5. The app will verify the connection and show the census tracker

---

## STEP 5 — Install on Phone Home Screen

Android (Chrome):
   1. Open the app in Chrome
   2. Tap the 3-dot menu
   3. Tap "Add to Home screen"
   4. Tap Add

iPhone (Safari):
   1. Open the app in Safari
   2. Tap the Share button
   3. Tap "Add to Home Screen"
   4. Tap Add

## Using the App

Voice Input (Recommended for field use):
   1. Tap the microphone button
   2. Speak the 4-digit PF number, e.g.:
      - "four four two zero" gives 4420
      - "PF four four two zero" gives 4420
      - "44 20" gives 4420
   3. The app automatically searches and shows the employee
   4. Tap "Mark as PRESENT"

Manual Input:
   1. Tap the number field
   2. Type the 4-digit PF number (e.g. 4420)
   3. Tap the Search button or press Enter
   4. Tap "Mark as PRESENT"

---

## Column Configuration (Pre-set for your sheet)

   Column A = PF Number
   Column E = Date and Time stamp
   Column F = PRESENT status

To change: tap the menu (top right) > Column Settings

---

## Troubleshooting

Problem: "Could not connect"
Solution: Check the Apps Script URL is correct and was deployed

Problem: "PF not found"
Solution: Check the PF number exists in Column A of your sheet

Problem: Voice not working
Solution: Use Chrome on Android or Safari on iPhone

Problem: Sheet not updating
Solution: Create a New Deployment in Apps Script after any code changes

Problem: Authorization error
Solution: Re-run authorization in Apps Script (Step 2, point 5)

---

IMPORTANT — Sheet Tab Name:
If your sheet tab at the bottom is NOT named "Sheet1", update line 4 in Code.gs:
   const SHEET_NAME = 'YourActualTabName';
Then create a New Deployment in Apps Script.
